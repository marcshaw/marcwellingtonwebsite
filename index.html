Sustainable Luxury

Crafted by Nature, Refined by Hand

Our wallets prove that luxury and sustainability are not mutually exclusive. Using pandan—a remarkable natural material that requires no chemicals to grow and leaves no trace when it returns to earth.

🌿

Naturally Renewable

Pandan regenerates quickly without replanting, growing wild in Thai wetlands with minimal water needs

♻️

Zero Waste Process

Every part of the pandan plant is used—leaves for weaving, scraps for compost, nothing goes to waste

🌍

Carbon Neutral

Hand-woven without machinery, air-dried naturally, and shipped plastic-free in recyclable packaging

⏳

Built to Last

Natural water resistance and durability means your wallet ages beautifully for years, then biodegrades completely
